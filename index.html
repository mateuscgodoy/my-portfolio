About Me

I was born in a small town in Brazil, where I lived almost my entire life until I went on to earn a degree in Food Engineering and while it's a wonderful field, I realized that it wasn't for me. It wasn't until two years ago that I had my first encounter with programming, but since then, I have been fully invested in it. Learning to code has been a transformative experience for me, and it's opened up a whole new world of possibilities.

I started my programming journey with C# and quickly became fascinated by game development. I spent countless hours studying and experimenting with Unity, and it was an incredibly rewarding experience. However, after nearly two years, I began to feel like I wanted to explore other possibilities. That's when I discovered the world of web development, and I knew I had found my calling. I've been diving deep into this fascinating field ever since, and I'm constantly amazed by all the amazing things that can be done with C# and the web.
